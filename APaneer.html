Aloo Paneer Recipe


    INGREDIENTS


    • Oil - 3 tbsp
    • Garlic - 10 cloves
    • Ginger - 20 gms (cut pcs)
    • Green Chili - 1 pc (cut)
    • Dry Red Chili - 2 pcs
    • Onion - 150 gms (cut)
    • Tomato - 100 gms (cut)
    • Oil - 3 tbsp
    • Cumin Seeds - 1 tsp
    • Gram Flour - 1 tbsp
    • Red Chili Powder - 1 tbsp
    • Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
    • Coriander-Cumin Powder - 1 tbsp
    • Salt - 1 tsp
    • Coriander - 1 cup (chopped)
    • Water - 2 glass
    • Green Peas - 50 gms
    • Paneer - 100 gms (cubes)

    INSTRUCTIONS


    • Steps for Matar Paneer
    • Heat oil in a deep vessel. Add garlic, ginger, green chilli, dry red chillies, and onion. Stir it.
    • Then add tomato and saute again. Cover it with a lid and cook for 10 minutes.
    • Transfer the ingredients after that into a mixer jar and grind it to a paste.
    • Again heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and temper them.Add gram flour and state it for a minute.
    • Then add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander-cumin powder, and salt. Remix everything.
    • Now add the paste and mix again. Also, add chopped coriander to it.
    • Then add water to it and stir. Add green peas and stir again. Cover it with a lid and cook for 10 minutes.
    • Add paneer cubes and stir once more. Cook for 5-7 minutes after covering it with a lid.
    • Matar Paneer is ready. Serve hot.
