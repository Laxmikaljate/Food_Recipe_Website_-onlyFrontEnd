VegFried Rice Recipe


    INGREDIENTS


    • 1 tbsp Oil
    • 1 tbsp Finely chopped Garlic
    • 1 tbsp Finely chopped Ginger
    • 1/2 cup Chopped Green beans
    • 1/2 cup Finely chopped Onion
    • Chopped Carrots
    • Chopped Baby corns
    • Sliced Green pepper
    • 1/4 cup Shredded Cabbage
    • 1 tsp Soya sauce
    • 1 tsp Tomato ketchup
    • 1/2 tsp Red chili sauce
    • 1/4 tsp Black pepper powder
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 cup Cooked rice
    • Finely chopped Spring onion


    INSTRUCTIONS


    • Heat up oil in a pan.
    • Add garlic, ginger and mix well.
    • Fry for about just 30 seconds.
    • Add beans and cook for about 2-3 minutes.
    • Add onion, carrots, baby corns and stir well.
    • Cook for 2 minutes more.
    • Add green pepper, and mix well.
    • Cook for one minute more.
    • Add cabbage, and mix well.
    • No need to cook more after adding cabbage as the crunch of veggies should be retained.
    • Add soya sauce, tomato ketchup, red chili sauce, black pepper powder and stir well.
    • Add salt and mix well. Add salt carefully as sauces already contain salt in them.
    • Add cooked rice and mix well.
    • You can use leftover rice too.
    • Add spring onion and fried rice is already.
