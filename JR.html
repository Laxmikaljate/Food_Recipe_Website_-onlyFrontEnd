Jira Rice Recipe


    INGREDIENTS


    • 1 cup Basmati Rice
    • 1 1/2 cups Water
    • 1 tsp Ghee
    • Salt to taste
    • 1 tsp Oil
    • Cumin seeds
    • Finely chopped Coriander leaves

    INSTRUCTIONS


    • Heat up a pan on medium heat.
    • Wash rice really well and soak it in enough water for 30 minutes.
    • Transfer the rice into the pan and add water.
    • Add salt to taste and mix well.
    • Let the rice come to boil on high heat.
    • When the rice begins to boil lower heat to medium and add ghee.
    • Cover on medium heat until 90% of moisture is absorbed in the rice for about 5 minutes.
    • Lower the heat to lowest and cook rice for 15 minutes more.
    • If rice sticks to the bottom of the pan, put a skillet on gas and put the pan on it and cook.
    • After 15 minutes, turn off the gas and let the pan be on the gas for 10 minutes more.
    • After 10 minutes, remove the lid and separate the rice with the help of a fork.
    • Heat up a pan on medium heat. Add oil. You can use butter or ghee instead of oil.
    • When the oil is enough hot, add cumin seeds and let them sizzle up.
    • Lower the heat to lowest. Add rice and coriander leaves.
    • Mix well and fry on low heat for about 3-4 minutes.
    • Jeera rice is already.
    • Jeera rice goes well with dal fry or dal tadka.
