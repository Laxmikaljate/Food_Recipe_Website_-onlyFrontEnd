Katachi Amti Recipe


    INGREDIENTS


    • 2 cups Dal Stock , water left over from cooking the chana dal for puran poli.
    • 1 cup Water , (or as required to thin down the stock)
    • 3 Cloves (Laung)
    • 1 to 2 Whole Black Peppercorns
    • 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds (Rai/ Kadugu)
    • 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds
    • 3 Cloves (Laung)
    • 1 to 2 Whole Black Peppercorns
    • 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds (Rai/ Kadugu)
    • 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera)
    • 1 Bay leaf (tej patta)
    • 5 to 6 Curry leaves , torn into pieces
    • 1 teaspoon Jaggery , grated (adjust the amount to taste)
    • 1 teaspoon Tamarind Paste , (adjust the amount to taste)

    INSTRUCTIONS


    • Rinse 1 cup chana dal (bengal gram) very well in water.
    • Then soak the chana dal in enough water for 1 to 2 hours.
    • Later drain the water. If you don’t have time then you can skip soaking chana dal.
    • In a pressure cooker, add the soaked chana dal.
    • Pour 3 cups of water.
    • Cook the chana dal for 6 to 7 whistles or till they are softened and cooked.
    • Once the pressure settles down on its own, then open the pressure cooker.
    • Strain the cooked dal. The dal has to be strained well. You can use a colander to strain the dal
      • .
    • Use the stock to make the katachi amti. Also reserve 2 tablespoons of the cooked chana dal.
    • The remaining chana dal is used to make bobbatlu or puran poli stuffing.
    • You can also make chana dal sundal or chana dal paratha from the cooked chana dal.
    • Measure and keep all the ingredients ready.
