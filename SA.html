Shenga Amti Recipe


    INGREDIENTS


    • 2 Drumsticks
    • 1 tsp Oil
    • Mustard seeds
    • 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
    • Curry leaves
    • 1 small or medium finely chopped Tomato
    • 1/2 tsp Garlic paste
    • 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
    • Turmeric powder
    • 1/2 tsp Red chili powder
    • 1/2 tsp Goda masala
    • 1/2 cup Water
    • 1 1/2 cups boiled Tur dal
    • Water
    • Salt to taste
    • Jaggery (Optional)
    • Finely chopped Coriander leaves

    INSTRUCTIONS


    • Cut drumsticks in pieces of about 2” and remove its skin.
    • Heat up oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them pop up.
    • Add cumin seeds and let it sizzle up.
    • Add curry leaves and tomato.
    • Add garlic paste, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder and goda masala.
    • Mix well and fry for about 2 minutes until garlic and other raw spices get absorbed in tomato.
    • Add drumsticks and water.
    • Cover and cook on medium heat for about 8-10 minutes until drumsticks are cooked well.
    • Drumsticks don’t take much time to cook.
    • Add boiled tur dal and water to adjust consistency.
    • Add salt and jiggery. You can skip jiggery if you don’t like it. Mix well.
    • Cover and boil amti on medium heat for about 5-8 minutes.
    • Add lots of finely chopped coriander leaves.
    • Turn off the gas and shevagyachya shenganchi amti is already.
    • This goes well with poli or bhakari.
    • It goes well with rice too
